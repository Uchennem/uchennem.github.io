Overview

Purpose

I believe websites should be simple and intuitive. Time should be spent making decisions about services on the website and not on trying to navigate the website. I make websites that are simple and intuitive to use. My purpose in creating this site is to offer an easily navigable website with great shoes at an affordable price. Life is a journey and each step you take matters. With The Shoemakers' shoes, you will never stop halfway. At The Shoemakers', you would find a shoe unique to your style and tailored for your feet, at a great quality.

Audience

Have you ever had to buy a shoe, and gotten your shoe size but it still does not fit? The Shoemakers' website targets those who want to get a shoe they are sure will fit every time. It is for those who want to set a trend and those who are tired of the regular. Shoes sold are for men and women of all ages. The shoemakers' site also focuses on delivering semi-casual to formal Shoes.

Why Choose The Shoemakers?
People will come to the site because we offer something different. Our Bespoke shoes are crafted with our customers in mind, with a quality that is world-class, and a price that would not empty their wallets. What we focus on is giving the best services at the best price. Life is a journey and with shoes from The Shomakers, you would never stop Halfway.

What will we Help you with?
One thing our site will do is help you find your style if you do not already know it, and for those who already do, we will help you live it. We offer a wide range of footwear, it does not matter if you don’t have a preference, you can choose a style you love from our selection, or we can build one together. Our goal is to give you a shoe that fits- both your feet and your taste.

What do our Customers need to know
The site is intuitive. You do not need to know much about shoes to use the site. They are different categories from gender-specific shoe category, to event-specific (semi-casual to formal). That is all the information you need for a good experience on the site. The reason you would come here is that you not only choose a shoe style but would be given an opportunity to speak with its maker. This is all to improve the “A Shoe by You and For You Experience”.
